Frequently Asked Questions

How does this program work?

During the first 3 months, you will learn everything from scratch about the field you are interested in through real world project building experience. Learning through this program is quite literally similar to learning on-the-job or learning through actual work experience.


After completing your 3 months On the Job training, you will be working on a Live Project for a period of 2 months with one of our 130+ partner startups and companies.

What will be my daily schedule? or What will be my daily engagement in the program?

You will be required to spend 1.5hrs / day for the first 3 months of your training building internship grade projects and learning concepts via multiformat training. During the 2 months live project, you will be required to spend a minimum of 3 hrs/day with the firm or startup with which you will be working.

Are there any pre-requisites? Can a beginner also enroll?

There are no pre-requisites for the program. The program is designed to take students from beginner to experts with 3 months intensive on the job training. All the concepts would be taught and covered from scratch.

What is the 100% Program Fee Refund Policy?

If you don't land a fully paid internship with stipend upto 15,000 or a Job with CTC upto 6lacs within 8 months of graduation from Trainity, we will refund your entire enrollment fee without any questions asked.

Will there be live classes and doubt support in the program?

Yes, there will be weekly mentor led live Q&A sessions to address your doubts along with 16 instructor led live simulated classes and two 1:1 mentor consultations during your 3 months training period.

What if my question was not answered by these FAQs?

We would recommend you to attend an introductory session where complete details and eligibility of the program is shared. If you are unable to attend the demo, you can click here for 1:1 mentor consultation : https://event.webinarjam.com/register/76/8q8lvb1p

How much stipend can I get during the internship?

Post completion of the program, you can get stipend upto ₹15,000/ month during internship depending on the company you will be working with. You will be eligible for paid internships only after successfully graduating from the 5 months program of Trainity. If you leave the program in between and do not complete, you will not be bound to guaranteed internship or placements.

What certificates will I receive?

During the course of program, you will receive 2 certificates from Trainity which inlcudes a "Training" completion certificate and an "Externship Certificate". Externship certificate is valid to be used as a form of work experience credential in resume and colleges.


You will also receive a "Live Project" and "Internship" certificate from the one of 130+ partner companies with which you will be working with during your live project and internship.

How does Trainity help us get Internships & Jobs?

Once you complete the 3+2 months program, we will provide exclusive employment assistance, which includes: i) Resume Building: A personal career coach is assigned to help you craft an effective resume. ii) Mock Interviews: Multiple mock interviews with industry experts will prepare you to be interview-ready. iii) Connecting with top companies: Help in securing interview invites at 300+ top partner companies to land your dream job.
