Placement T&C

Hey there ,

Trainity welcomes you. We want to lend you all of our support in making the shift to your selected domain a success.

The Trainity Course Job/Internships Opportunity Guarantee (the “Trainity Guarantee") is provided to you subject to the terms and conditions listed below.

We want you to share responsibility for your career transition as well. To be eligible for this assurance, you must meet the following criteria. You may still complete the course and gain all of the benefits of placement training if these standards are not satisfied, but you will not be eligible for the opportunities granted under this assurance.

  • Requirements for Eligibility

    • You must be at least 18 years old.
    • By the day you are permitted to complete the Course, you must have a Bachelor's Degree in any topic from an accredited educational institution. In addition to the essential skills and experience you will gain during this Course, most employers still require a Bachelor's degree.
    • If you are still in your final or third year of your Bachelor's degree when you enrol in the Trainity Course, you must not have any detentions, backlogs, or other issues that might prevent you from working or interning once the Course is completed.
    • You must be able to communicate in both oral and written English.
    • After successfully finishing the Course, you must be eligible to work legally in India.
    • Any background checks related to jobs/internships that you apply for must be passed. Without limiting the above, you will not be eligible for this guarantee if you are unable to get a job/internship offer due to your inability to pass a background check related to the job offer.
    • Only internships will be recognised as Qualifying chances for applicants who are still in their final or third year of their Bachelor's degree at the time of enrolling in the Trainity course.

  • Qualifying Opportunities

    Definitions

    1. A salaried or waged employee who works at least 30 hours each week on average; or
    2. A full-time contractor for at least three months ; or
    3. An intern in a paid or unpaid internship who is being evaluated for a full-time salaried position; or
    4. A freelancing job that pays part-time or full-time.

    The Qualifying Opportunity that you pick, will be agreed upon between you and the Trainity team.

  • Guarantee Period

    The "Guarantee Term" is defined as the time between the completion of your 3-month Course and the receiving of a Course certificate of completion, or a longer period if we've mutually agreed to extend it. Despite the preceding, Trainity reserves the right to prolong the Guarantee Period at its discretion:

    (A) by one month in case seasonal hiring slowdowns.
    or
    (B) by up to size months if a natural disaster or unforseen event occurs beyound Trainity's control.

    The Trainity Guarantee is only valid in India. Trainity , on the other hand, does not promise a position in a specific city or location. Candidates must be willing to relocate for jobs or internships.

  • Commitment from the Candidate

    To properly transition you into your Trainity course profession, we demand equal efforts and commitment from you.

    Hence, in order for the Trainity Guarantee to be applicable:

    You must have completed all of the graduation requirements, which include:

    • You must finish 80 percent of the Course within 100 days of the commencement date of your Course, excluding any breaks granted to you by Trainity.
    • You must successfully complete all of the projects in your Course.
    • You must achieve or exceed expectations in all projects and tasks that have been approved by the Trainity (as per the Review criteria). You may submit revised versions depending on feedback until you satisfy expectations.
    • You must complete and pass all placement training assignments that were either (a) stated in the curriculum in the order as they appear in the curriculum, or (b) directly assigned to you. This includes, but is not limited to, participating in all necessary calls and mock interviews.

    After completing the aforementioned essential conditions, the 6-month guarantee term begins on the day you are approved for completion.

    • You must respond to Trainity's placement-related contacts within 72 hours.
    • To achieve a Qualifying Opportunity, you must constantly make reasonable and good faith attempts.

  • How This Guarantee Applies To You

    Without limiting the preceding, situations that render this guarantee null and void include, but are not limited to :

    1. You decline a job offer in exchange for a Qualifying Opportunity.
    2. You decide not to search for jobs.
    3. You decide to look for a job that does not satisfy the aforementioned Qualifying Opportunity requirements or is not in the Trainity field/industry.
    4. You take a position that does not fulfil the above-mentioned Qualifying Opportunity requirements or is not in the Trainity field/industry before the 6-month guarantee term expires.
    5. You do not want to or are unable to live and work in one of the cities with Qualifying Opportunity positions.
    6. You do not interact with Trainity on a continuous basis during your search, including informing us of any offers you receive.
    7. You lose your work authorization in India or do not have enough work authorization to satisfy the conditions outlined above during your search, even though you expected to have authorization or had suitable work authorization at one point during your Course.
    8. During the Course or guarantee term, you become physically or psychologically unable to undertake a successful job search as indicated above.
    9. Throughout the 6-month placement period, you do not apply for Qualifying Opportunities in the cities where positions are available, as specified above
    10. You do not complete the interview process for Qualifying Positions in a timely and professional manner, including but not limited to not participating in the interview process as expected by the employer by responding to employer communications, showing up on time for interviews, and providing documents or following up as expected by employers.
    11. You no-show or reschedule/cancel a call with a career counsellor or mock interviewer three or more times with less than 24 hours notice.
